# Phase 1: Primal Queen Structural Extraction

## Section Inventory

| # | Section Name | Primary Job | Component Type | Copy Length |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Top Nav** | Navigation, brand presence | Horizontal nav bar with logo, cart, product callouts | Nav links only |
| 2 | **Hero** | Hook attention, state problem, promise solution | Full-width centered text + product image + social proof bar + guarantee badges | Headline (~12 words, bold caps) + subhead (2 sentences) + CTA + social proof line + 3 badge labels |
| 3 | **Testimonial Strip 1** | Immediate social proof | Horizontal testimonial cards with customer photos | 5 short quotes (1-2 sentences each) with names |
| 4 | **Flash Sale Banner** | Urgency | Scrolling marquee with countdown timer | Single repeating phrase + timer |
| 5 | **Problem / Education** | Problem agitation, build belief in mechanism | Full-width editorial text block | Headline + 4-5 body paragraphs |
| 6 | **Scientific Article** | Deep education, credibility, mechanism explanation | Long-form article with subheadings, body text, references | Very long (~3000-5000 words), authored by named doctor, 57 citations |
| 7 | **Quality Badges** | Trust, quality assurance | Icon grid (6 icons) + manufacturing certification badges | Short labels under each icon + facility callout |
| 8 | **Scarcity Alert** | Urgency, scarcity | Warning/alert callout box | Single paragraph |
| 9 | **Testimonial Strip 2** | Reinforce social proof | Customer testimonial cards with photos and names | 5 detailed testimonials (3-4 sentences each) |
| 10 | **Founder Story** | Brand connection, relatability | Narrative text + product imagery + organ/ingredient icons | Long-form personal story (~800-1200 words), ingredient feature list |
| 11 | **Results Timeline** | Set expectations, handle "how long" objection | Vertical timeline / stepped layout | 6 timepoints with headline + 1-2 sentence description each |
| 12 | **How to Use** | Reduce friction, show simplicity | 3-step horizontal layout with step images | 3 short instruction blocks (headline + 1 sentence each) |
| 13 | **Ingredient Comparison** | Demonstrate ingredient superiority | Comparison cards (product vs. alternative) | 6 comparison points with multiplier stats |
| 14 | **Manufacturing Transparency** | Trust, quality sourcing | Text block + certification badge images | Sourcing details + processing method (~2 paragraphs) |
| 15 | **Pricing / Purchase** | Convert | Two pricing cards side-by-side + countdown timer + value stacking | Pricing details, bonus item list, subscription terms |
| 16 | **FAQ** | Objection handling | Accordion (expand/collapse) | 13 Q&A pairs |
| 17 | **Footer** | Legal, navigation, trust | Standard footer with links and disclaimers | Links, legal text, contact info |

## Design Token Extraction

| Token | Primal Queen Value | Restural Adaptation |
|---|---|---|
| Primary color | Pink/coral (#E8637A approx) | Forest green (#2D8F4E) |
| Secondary color | Dark text / near-black | Dark navy (#1B3A4B) |
| Accent color | Gold/warm tone | Light green tint (#E8F5EC) |
| Background treatment | White + cream alternating sections | Off-white (#F9F8F5) + white alternating |
| Heading font | Bold serif display (caps) | Playfair Display 700 |
| Body font | Clean sans-serif | DM Sans 400/500 |
| Button style | Pill / rounded, filled, bold text | Pill / rounded, filled green, white text |
| Image treatment | Contained product shots, lifestyle photos | Contained product shots, medical/neural imagery |
| Visual rhythm | Spacious — generous padding between sections | Spacious — matching rhythm |

## Conversion Structure

| Element | Position(s) in Page | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary CTA** | Hero (#2), after Education (#5), after Article (#6), after Founder Story (#10), Pricing (#15), after FAQ (#16) | ~6 placements, PQ repeats "UNLOCK YOUR TRUE POWER" |
| **Social proof** | Hero bar (#2), Testimonials 1 (#3), Testimonials 2 (#9), customer count in hero | Rating + count in hero, testimonial strips at 2 depths |
| **Risk reversal** | Hero badges (#2), Pricing cards (#15), FAQ (#16) | 365-day guarantee, free shipping, cancel anytime |
| **Urgency / scarcity** | Flash Sale Banner (#4), Scarcity Alert (#8), Pricing countdown (#15) | Timer + stock warning + monthly selling-out claim |
| **Mechanism** | Education section (#5), Scientific Article (#6) | Two-layer: accessible narrative then deep science |
| **Objection handling** | FAQ (#16), Comparison (#13), Timeline (#11) | FAQ is primary; comparison handles "why not just eat X"; timeline handles "how long" |
